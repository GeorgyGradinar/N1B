— 03 / Process

Four moves.
No surprises.

— Typically 6–12 wks
01

Discovery

One careful week. We listen, we read, we ask the questions you wish your last agency had. Output: a written brief.

— wk 01
02

Direction

Two or three studied directions, presented in person. We commit to one and develop it deeply rather than show twenty thin ideas.

— wks 02–04
03

Build

Design and engineering by the same team. Weekly Friday demos. You see the work grow, not just the deck.

— wks 04–10
04

Launch & Care

Quiet launch. Then we stay — for revisions, additions, and the next chapter. Most of our clients are still our clients.

— 06 / Questions

Frequently asked.

— 04
— 01Who do you work with?+
Independent companies who care about design. Mostly hospitality, lifestyle, publishing, architecture and culture — but the vertical matters less than the ambition. We've turned down briefs that didn't fit.
— 02How long does a project take?+
Typically 6 to 12 weeks. A focused identity might be 4 weeks; a full brand-and-site engagement is closer to 10. We agree the timeline upfront and move at a steady, weekly pace.
— 03What does it cost?+
Identity engagements typically start at $14k. Full brand & site projects start at $32k. We send a fixed estimate before you sign anything — no clock-watching, no billable surprises.
— 04Can we hire you for just one part?+
Yes. Plenty of our work is "site only" or "identity only". We just ask for time to get to know your business — short briefs make weak design.
